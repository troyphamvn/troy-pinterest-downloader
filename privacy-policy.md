# Privacy Policy — Troy Pinterest Downloader

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**Developer:** Troy Pham

---

## Overview

Troy Pinterest Downloader is a Chrome extension that allows users to download images, GIFs, and videos from Pinterest in their original quality. This policy explains how the extension handles data.

---

## Data Collection

**Troy Pinterest Downloader does not collect any personal data.**

The extension does not:
- Collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- Track browsing history or activity
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Require user accounts or login

---

## What the Extension Does

- Detects Pinterest pin cards on `pinterest.com` to display a download button
- Resolves original-quality media URLs from Pinterest's CDN (`i.pinimg.com`)
- Saves files directly to your local device via the Chrome Downloads API
- Stores your UI preferences (hover mode / click-anywhere mode) locally in `chrome.storage.sync` — this data never leaves your device

---

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `downloads` | Save media files to your device |
| `storage` | Remember your extension preferences locally |
| `pinterest.com` | Inject the download button on Pinterest pages |
| `i.pinimg.com` | Access original-quality media URLs from Pinterest's CDN |

No data is shared with, sold to, or transferred to any third party under any circumstance.
